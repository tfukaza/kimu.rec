The hardest thing about jeans is finding a pair that does not feel like a compromise. Most comfortable jeans look too casual, and most jeans that look clean enough for work feel stiff by the end of the day. The sweet spot is a pair that moves like lounge clothing while still reading visually as normal denim.

UNIQLO's EZY Ultra Stretch Jeans are the best pick because they are probably the most comfortable jeans in this category while still looking presentable. They have enough structure to work with a casual button-down, sweater, or simple jacket, but they do not have the rigid, restrictive feel that makes many jeans annoying for long days. That makes them especially useful when you want one pair of pants that can handle errands, travel, casual offices, and relaxed business-casual settings without needing to think too hard.
