As the saying goes, cooking is an art, while baking is chemistry. 
Making the best grilled cuisine requires precise temperature control, which can be hard to perfect, let alone "set and forget".
This immediately limits us to smart ovens which can sync the heating control to internal temperature of the food.

The Typhur Dome 2 in particular stands out because it has heating elements placed close together both on the top and bottom.
So with the proper settings you can actually cook a whole steak or fish without flipping it.
Of course, that means you only get sear marks on one side, 
but we think it's a worthy tradeoff to get perfectly cooked food in one go.
