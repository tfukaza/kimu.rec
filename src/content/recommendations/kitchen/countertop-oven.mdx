A countertop oven is best when it reduces how much attention cooking requires. For proteins, thermometer support is useful because it lets you cook to doneness instead of guessing from time alone. Self-cleaning also matters because grease and residue build up quickly around high-heat air-fryer heating elements.

The Typhur Dome 2 is the best fit here because it combines strong raw cooking performance with a much more set-and-forget workflow than a normal air fryer. The wide cooking surface, dual heating, and self-cleaning mode make it easier to use often, and it is especially effective at making delicious grilled-style food without needing to constantly babysit the cook.
