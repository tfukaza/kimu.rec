The best sink caddy is one that keeps wet tools inside the sink instead of letting them drip across the counter. It also needs to be compact enough that it does not make the sink harder to use.

The simplehuman Slim Sink Caddy works because it is small, stable, and easy to remove when the sink needs cleaning. The combination of suction cups and a ledge hanger makes it feel more secure than a caddy that relies on only one attachment point.
