Reverse osmosis is the main thing to look for if the goal is noticeably better-tasting water. Instead of relying on a simple inline filter, an RO system pushes water through a fine membrane, which can remove more of the dissolved material that affects taste.

The Waterdrop K6 is a strong pick because it solves two kitchen problems at once. It filters water and dispenses hot water from the same under-sink system, which is useful if you make tea, cook with hot water often, or want fewer separate appliances competing for space.
