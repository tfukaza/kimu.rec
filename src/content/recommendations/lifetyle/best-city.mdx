New York is the best city because it rewards curiosity at every scale. You can build a day around a world-class museum, a neighborhood restaurant, a long walk, a specific train ride, or one person you wanted to meet, and it all feels equally valid.

## Things to consider

- The density is the point: more people, more scenes, more food, and more chances for something interesting to happen.
- Public transit makes the city feel playable without needing a car.
- The tradeoff is friction. New York is expensive, crowded, loud, and often inconvenient, but that is part of why the ceiling is so high.
