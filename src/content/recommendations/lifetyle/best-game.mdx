Counter-Strike is the best game because it is almost impossibly durable. The rules are simple enough to understand immediately, but the ceiling is basically endless: aim, economy, utility, positioning, timing, nerves, and team trust all matter.

## Things to consider

- It is readable: the important decisions are visible, round by round.
- It is social: one clutch, one bad call, or one perfect flash can define the whole session.
- It keeps proving the same point across versions: the core design is strong enough to survive decades of changing graphics, maps, metas, and players.
