The best air purifier setup is two machines, not one. Use the Austin Air Bedroom Machine for VOCs, odors, gases, formaldehyde, and chemical pollutants, then pair it with the Levoit Core 600S-P for dust, pollen, pet dander, and everyday particle cleanup.

- Choose the Austin Air side of the setup when VOC, odor, smoke, gas, and chemical reduction matter as much as particle filtration.
- Choose the Levoit side for high-airflow daily cleaning, visible dust, pollen seasons, pet dander, and auto mode.
- Give both units breathing room. The Austin Air uses 360-degree intake, and the Levoit works best when it can circulate air through the room instead of being buried behind furniture.
