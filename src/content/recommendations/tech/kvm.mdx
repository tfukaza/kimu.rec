The StarTech.com P2CDD143-KVM-SWITCH is the KVM pick because reliability matters more than novelty here. A KVM is infrastructure: if it drops video, fails to wake a keyboard, or needs constant reconnecting, the entire desk setup feels broken.

This model is the practical choice because it is built like a work tool instead of a gadget. It supports two DisplayPort-enabled computers, dual DisplayPort monitors up to 8K 60Hz, two USB 10Gbps hub ports, four USB HID ports, and switching by push button or hotkeys.

## Things to consider

- Choose this when reliability is the deciding factor and you want the KVM to disappear into the setup.
- Make sure both computers support DisplayPort 1.4 and DSC if you want the maximum video performance.
- It is DisplayPort-first, so do not buy it for an HDMI-only desk without planning the adapter chain carefully.
- It does not require drivers or software, which is exactly what you want from desk infrastructure.
